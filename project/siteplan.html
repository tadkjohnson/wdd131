Table Top Gaming Club

Purpose of a gaming club

A place to gather that enjoy gaming on a table top. None are exluded, but video gamers will find difficulties as we dont have that kind of a set up. We are a Table Top Gaming Club. We play games on a table top.

Our group of people are never the same ones, but are simply the type that spend there time imaging another life, another world, or being a hero in a world of thier making. We spend weekends at a local game shop battling, talking, laughing, and having a good time. During the week, outside of work or school, we maybe found gaming with our familys, finding and trying new games. All are welcome to join us.

The purpose of our club is to provide a place where all can play, feel welcome, and have a good time. The club will provide instruction if you want to learn a specific game, or provide a place for you to watch and learn.
