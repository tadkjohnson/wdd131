Brief Description of where we play.

Growing up the group I played with played at any ones house who's parents would allow us to gather and play at. As I aged, I discovered game stores that had tables we
could play at, and then expantion of games began. There are stores with flat tables perfect for paper games, like Rifts, Dungeons & Dragons, Battle Tech, and a few
thousand others. Or there are tables with make shift war scenes, forests, citys, ruins, and any other object found in the world of fantasy gaming, just to allow a few thousand
games to be played on it.

My house, we have a few tables to play around, just enough for the 4-6 of us, but if need be we have a few tables we can pull out in to the garage or spare room to play. At
Nerdvana, there are a dozen flat tables to gather around. At Canada's there are less tables, more rooms, and many more models to build your perfect arena for the battle. The
only real limits are your imagination, time, and of course finances.

Please reach out if you would like to join us, watch, play, or learn.
