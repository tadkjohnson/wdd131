Brief Description of Where We Play

Growing up, we played at any friend's house whose parents allowed us to gather and game. As we grew older, we discovered game stores with tables — and our game library grew.

Some stores offer flat tables for games like Rifts, Dungeons & Dragons, BattleTech, and many others. Others feature elaborate terrain setups — forests, cities, ruins — bringing war scenes to life for miniature games.

At my house, we have a few small tables for up to 6 players. If needed, we bring out folding tables for the garage or spare rooms. Nerdvana has about a dozen flat tables, while Canada's has more rooms and an impressive collection of models to help you build the perfect battlefield.

The only real limits are your imagination, time, and of course, finances.
