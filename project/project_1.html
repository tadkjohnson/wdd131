This is some of the games we play!


We play taple top games this includes: Dungeon & Dragons, Magic The Gathering, Pokemon, Wingspan, Munchkin, Dominion, Marvel, Starwars, Startrek, HeroScape, Warhammer: 40,000, Warhammer: Fantasy Battle, SmallWorld, Ticket to Ride, Talisman, Azul, Splendor, Settlers of Catan, and a few thousand more. Just to name a few.

You will notice most of the games are among friends, we talk, we banter, we help each other, it is a casual game, where the primary object is to have fun. If your into serious games, there are tournements, which have minimal fees, are fun as well, but tend to draw the serious.

People will wonder who is invited. We solemnly say everyone is invited, come one and come all. The more the merrier. Most games are simple 2-3 players, but many can handle 5-7 players. People show up we will accomodate. There are those that like to teach, and those that like to watch, but there is always a empty chair for future gamers.

This is the place life long friends are created, familys gather, alliances are made, wars are waged, and everyone leaves a victor of having fun. Some win, and others lose, but the overall desire for all is to enjoy thier time here. Much like any other gathering spot, there are all kinds that meet with us; there are doctors, lawyers, buisness owners, rich, and poor, but to look over the group they are all here enjoying the game.
