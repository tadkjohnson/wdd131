Welcome To the Table Top Gamers Club

This is the club people go if they enjoy a good game. You can watch, you can learn, you can participate, and you can take part. Join us, it costs notihng, and you might meet and make the best friends of yourl ife. We include a good game, with good people, and the best of times.

We play taple top games this includes: Dungeon & Dragons, Magic The Gathering, Pokemon, Wingspan, Munchkin, Dominion, Marvel, Starwars, Startrek, HeroScape, Warhammer: 40,000, Warhammer: Fantasy Battle, SmallWorld, Ticket to Ride, Talisman, Azul, Splendor, Settlers of Catan, and a few thousand more. Just to name a few.

You will notice most of the games are among friends, we talk, we banter, we help each other, it is a casual game, where the primary object is to have fun. If your into serious games, there are tournements, which have minimal fees, are fun as well, but are for the serious minded folks.

People will wonder who is invited. We solemnly say everyone is invited, come one and come all. The more the merrier. Most games are simple 2-3 players, but many can handle 5-7 players. People show up we will accomodate. There are those that like to teach, and those that like to watch, but there is always a empty chair for future gamers.
